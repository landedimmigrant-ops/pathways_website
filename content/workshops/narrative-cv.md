Narrative CV development helps you shape clear, evidence-based descriptions of your contributions and the significance of your work for funding and assessment contexts.

## What you'll get
- A workable structure for your narrative sections
- Guidance on choosing evidence that supports your claims
- A clearer story of contribution, context, and significance

## Who it's for
Researchers preparing applications that require a narrative CV (or revising how they describe impact and contributions).

## How to prepare
Bring a draft CV (or notes) plus 2-3 contributions you want to highlight.

## Outcomes
You leave with a clearer draft and a plan for revision.
