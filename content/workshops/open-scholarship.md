Open Science and Open Scholarship practices helps you plan practical ways to share methods, outputs, and research processes with transparency and integrity.

## What you'll get
- A clear set of open scholarship actions matched to your project stage
- Guidance on selecting sharing routes for methods, data, and outputs
- Advice on balancing openness with ethical and legal constraints

## Who it's for
Researchers who want to strengthen openness in ongoing or completed projects.

## How to prepare
Bring a short overview of your project and any current sharing requirements.

## Outcomes
You leave with a practical plan for applying open scholarship practices.
