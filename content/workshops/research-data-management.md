Research Data Management guidance supports you in planning how research data will be organized, documented, protected, and shared across the project lifecycle.

## What you'll get
- A practical approach to structuring and documenting project data
- Guidance on storage, access, and retention planning
- Support for aligning data practices with policy and ethics expectations

## Who it's for
Researchers preparing grants or running projects that involve active data collection and management.

## How to prepare
Bring your draft data management plan (if available) and a short description of your data types.

## Outcomes
You leave with clearer data management priorities and next steps.
